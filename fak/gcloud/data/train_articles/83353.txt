Obamacare: Following financial losses, Aetna will not sell Affordable Care Act plans in Florida for 2017 | Miami Herald
A 2014 photo of a sign for Aetna Inc. atop a building at the company headquarters in in Hartford, Conn. Health insurer Aetna will become the latest health insurer to reduce participation in the Affordable Care Act’s insurance exchanges when it trims its presence to four states for 2017 — down from 15 this year. Aetna will not sell ACA plans in Florida next year. Jessica Hill AP
