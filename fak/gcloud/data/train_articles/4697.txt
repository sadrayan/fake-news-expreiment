2013 WISCONSIN ACT 46

The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:

2013 Wisconsin Act 20 ] Section 9134 (4L) (g) 1. Notwithstanding paragraph (b) 1., if a school board levied more for community programs and services in 2011 than it levied for community programs and services in 2012, its limit under paragraph (b) 1. is the amount it levied for community programs and services in 2011, except as provided in subdivision 2.

2. Subdivision 1. applies only if the school board's levy for community programs and services in 2011 or 2012 was less than $1,000,000.

46,1m Section 1m . Nonstatutory provisions.

(1) (a) Notwithstanding section 121.15 (4) (b) of the statutes, the state superintendent of public instruction shall calculate the total amount of state aid that each school district will receive in the 2013-14 school year by October 31, 2013, instead of on October 15, 2013.

(b) Notwithstanding section 120.12 (3) and (4) of the statutes, for 2013 the references to November 1 in those sections are changed to November 6.

(c) Notwithstanding section 119.16 (8) (b) of the statutes, for 2013 the reference in that section to the first Monday in August is changed to November 6.

(d) Notwithstanding section 121.90 (2) (am) 1. of the statutes, for 2013 the reference to October 15 in that section is changed to October 31.

46,2 Section 2 . Fiscal changes.

(1) In the schedule under section 20.005 (3) of the statutes for the appropriation to the department of public instruction under section 20.255 (2) (ac) of the statutes, as affected by the acts of 2013, the dollar amount is increased by $40,000,000 for the first fiscal year of the fiscal biennium in which this subsection takes effect to increase funding for the purposes for which the appropriation is made. In the schedule under section 20.005 (3) of the statutes for the appropriation to the department of public instruction under section 20.255 (2) (ac) of the statutes, as affected by the acts of 2013, the dollar amount is increased by $60,000,000 for the second fiscal year of the fiscal biennium in which this subsection takes effect to increase funding for the purposes for which the appropriation is made.
