Pres. Jonathan Visits Baga, Borno State & Mubi in Adamawa
President Goodluck Jonathan on Thursday visited Baga town in Borno State, and Mubi in Adamawa.

During his visit, he addressed officers and men of the Nigerian army in both towns, which were recently recaptured from Boko Haram,Vanguard reports.

He applauded the army for their bravery and patriotism they have shown in the fight against Boko Haram.

Jonathan also stated that their performance so far proves beyond reasonable doubt that they are more than capable of defeating Boko Haram.
