Immanuel Bible Church
REACHING OUR COMMUNITY WITH THE POWER OF THE GOSPEL Immanuel has a wide range of ministries and programs that touch the lives of children, students, single and married adults. We have outreach ministries to our local community as well as a global reach through our World Missions. Stop by this Sunday, we would love to meet you. We are conveniently located in Springfield, Virginia, just minutes from the Capital Beltway at the intersection of Braddock and Backlick Roads.
