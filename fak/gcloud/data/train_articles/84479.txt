Travis County taxable home values rise 8%
Propelled by job and population growth, the market value of homes in Travis County jumped 12.6 percent on average for 2014, chief appraiser Marya Crigler said Thursday.

The taxable residential values — a home’s market value minus exemptions — rose an average of 8 percent, Crigler said. That rate will be used by the county, cities, school districts and other local taxing units to set 2014 property tax rates.
