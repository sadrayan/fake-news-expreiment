On May 1, 2019, at McLane Stadium in Waco, the Western District of Texas held the investiture ceremony for the Honorable Alan D. Albright, U.S. District Judge for the Waco Division. The video for the event is posted at Investiture of the Honorable Alan D. Albright .

Chief Judge Garcia has signed the Special Order Concerning Admission of Attorneys which extends the period of time for which the 2019 Federal Court Practice course may be used when applicants are applying to the bar of this court. The entire course must be completed no later than May 31, 2020 in order to fulfill the educational requirements for admission set forth in W.D. Tex. L.R. AT-1(b)(2).

Please review the order for additional details.
