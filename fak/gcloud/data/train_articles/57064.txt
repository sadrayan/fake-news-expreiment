The 10 Most Educated States in the U.S.
As students across the country graduate from college this month, they must decide not only what career they want to pursue, but where they wish to pursue it.

States with the most-educated populations not only draw recent graduates from other regions, but they also retain students from their own college systems.

In a recent ranking, U.S. News determined the states with the highest educational attainment, measured by the percentage of people 25 years and older with an associate degree or higher in 2016.

Many of the most-educated states do not rank well overall for higher education because of their high tuition and student debt.
