Kyle Pomerleau is Chief Economist and Vice President of Economic Analysis at the Tax Foundation. In his current position he leads the tax modeling team, oversees the research of the Center for Economic Analysis, and researches and writes on a variety of federal tax issues. His work has been cited in most major media outlets throughout the country. Kyle is originally from Maine and holds an MPP from the McCourt School of Public Policy at Georgetown University. He lives in Arlington, Virginia with his lovely wife, Shuting.
