Manufacturing tax credit, DOT audit are featured topics on Capit - WREX.com – Rockford’s News Leader
MADISON (WKOW) -- Wisconsin's manufacturing and agricultural tax credit and the recent audit report on the Department of Transportation were the topics of discussion on this weekend's edition of Capital City Sunday.



State Representative Gordon Hintz (D-Oshkosh) and Scott Manley, Vice-President of Government Relations for Wisconsin Manufacturers and Commerce, were the featured guests.



This edition of the show originally aired on January 29.
