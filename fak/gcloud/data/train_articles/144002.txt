Posta launches stamp in rhino protection drive
Tourism and Wildlife secretary Najib Balala will launch the stamp at the Ol Pejeta Conservancy in Nanyuki, in collaboration with the Kenya Wildlife Service. Posta in a statement yesterday said that the stamp is intended to raise awareness on the plight of the Rhino as a way of highlighting the importance of conserving wildlife. The stamp comes after the death of Sudan, the last male white rhino in March this year.
