The Muslim Prayer and Reading Room is located in MURRAY DODGE HALL on the 3rd floor.

Prayer room for 5-daily prayers and can always be used as a place for quiet reflection.



Students are welcome to borrow books, and sign them out. We have books ranging from Qur'anic commentaries and collections of Hadith to law and spirituality. Enjoy!

Directions to Murray-Dodge Hall

Easily accessible from Nassau Street, Murray Dodge is located diagonal from the Chapel and behind East Pyne Hall. Public parking is available in Lot 23 in the evening and in Lot 10 on the weekend as well as metered parking and private garages in town. Please use this map for the exact location.
