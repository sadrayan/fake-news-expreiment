Newsmakers 4/18/2014: Robert Walsh, NEARI
EAST PROVIDENCE, R.I. (WPRI) – This week on Newsmakers: Robert Walsh, Executive Director of teacher’s union National Education Association of Rhode Island (NEARI). Fresh off the collapse of the pension settlement, Walsh talks about where he sees the case going and discusses the controversial voting process. With the NEARI supporting gubernatorial candidate Clay Pell, Walsh talks about how he sees Pell winning the Democratic primary despite lagging in the polls. Walsh also reacts to the federal investigation swirling around former House Speaker Gordon Fox.
