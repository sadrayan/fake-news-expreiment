Florida woos private flood insurance, but trickle likely
Florida and flood insurance

•Florida has nearly 40 percent of the nation’s policie, more than 2 million.

•Most policies are with the National Flood Insurance Program. Private carriers provide a limited number of policies or supplemental coverage.

•Floridians have paid $16.1 billion in federal premiums over several decades while collecting just $3.7 billion in claims.

•Palm Beach County has more than 162,000 flood policies. More could be added as federal flood maps are redrawn.
