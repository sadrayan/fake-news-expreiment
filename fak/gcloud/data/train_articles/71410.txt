Best Babies Zone
Mission

To give every baby born in a Best Babies Zone the best chance in life.

Every baby is born healthy, into communities that enable them to thrive and reach their full potential.

Best Babies Zone communities promote good health from one generation to the next. Positive life experiences and increased opportunities where we live, work, learn and play, such as early childhood education, affordable housing and a vibrant local economy will help everyone thrive and be healthy.
