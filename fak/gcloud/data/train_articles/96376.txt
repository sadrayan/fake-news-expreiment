Mike McCabe discusses potential run for Governor on Capital City - WKOW 27: Madison, WI Breaking News, Weather and Sports
MADISON (WKOW) -- Blue Jean Nation founder Mike McCabe talked about a potential 2018 run for Wisconsin Governor on this week's edition of Capital City Sunday.



McCabe, the former executive director of the campaign finance watchdog group Wisconsin Democracy Campaign, said he hopes to announce a run after Labor Day. McCabe said he is still trying to decide whether to run as a Democrat or an Independent.



Joe Kirgues, co-founder of gener8tor - a Wisconsin business accelerator group - was also on the program to discuss Wisconsin ranking last in the United States for entrepreneurship for the third straight year.



This edition of Capital City Sunday originally aired on May 21.
