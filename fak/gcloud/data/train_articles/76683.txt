Chapter 50, Ethics Commission Rules
TEXAS ETHICS COMMISSION RULES

Chapter 50. LEGISLATIVE SALARIES AND PER DIEM

ยง 50.1. Legislative Per Diem

(a) The legislative per diem is $221. The per diem is intended to be paid to each member of the legislature and the lieutenant governor for each day during the regular session and for each day during any special session.

(b) If necessary, this rule shall be applied retroactively to ensure payment of the $221 per diem for 2019.
