Fast Food Menu Prices
McDonald’s is the largest hamburger fast food chain in the world serving food in about 120 countries. The company was founded in 1940 by two brothers, Richard McDonald and Maurice McDonald. Today, McDonalds prices are very competitive with the rest of the fast food industry.

McDonalds sells many types of foods including hamburgers, french fries, chicken, cheeseburgers, soft drinks, and salads. They also have a value menu which sells products for only $1 a piece.
