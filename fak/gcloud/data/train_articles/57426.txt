Modern Slavery Facts
SUSANA TRIMARCO

Trafficking for Sexual Exploitation

Argentina

Susana is the mother of Marita Veron who disappeared on April 3 2002. Marita is believed to have been kidnapped by a powerful human trafficking network. Trimarco has spent years searching for her daughter, who is still missing. In December 2013 ten people were convicted of the kidnapping and sexual exploitation of Marita Veron. Susan Trimarco is the founder of the Maria de los Angeles Foundation which rescues victims of trafficking. Her high profile campaigns have changed the law.
