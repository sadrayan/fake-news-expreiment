two-striped telamonia spider (telamonia dimidiata)
Male

Female Classification: Family Salticidae, Jumping Spiders

Habitat: Foliage in wooded environments.

Female: 9-11mm.

Male: 8-9 mm.

Distribution: Singapore (new record), Indonesia, India. Legs I and II of the males are armed with thick brushes of bristles. This species is cited in the toilet seat spider email, which is a hoax originating in 1999.
