At least 28 were killed in an explosion in Mar Girgis church in Egypt
Description

epa05899111 A man holds a Coptic clergy clothes with blood stains on them at Mar Girgis church after a bomb explosion, Tanta, 90km north of Cairo, Egypt, 09 April 2017. According to the Egyptian Health Ministry, at least 28 were killed and 71 injured in a bomb explosion at Mar Girgis Coptic church in the central delta city of Tanta during the Palm Sunday mass.
