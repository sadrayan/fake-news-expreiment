Maine Question 4 — Increase Minimum Wage — Results: Approved
Voters approved Question 4 — Increase Minimum Wage — in Maine on Tuesday. The measure calls for increasing the state’s minimum wage to $12 per hour by 2020 from $7.50. Maine’s minimum wage exceeds the federal floor of $7.25. Gov. Paul R. LePage, a Republican, has said that business owners could suffer from higher minimum wages.
