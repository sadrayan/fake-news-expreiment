Get That Retro iOS '86 Theme for Your Jailbroken iPhone Now
Last week we fawned over the wonderfully nostalgic iOS '86 theme someone had created for their iPhone. This week, that theme is available for download. If you have a jailbroken iPhone, that is.

To get the theme, just fire up Cydia and add the repository http://repo.ithemes.it. Once you do that, you'll be able to download the theme and apply it using Winterboard. [iSpazio via AppAdvice via Cult of Mac]
