Paul Wellstone Mental Health and Addiction Equity Act of 2007 Rep Kennedy, Patrick J. [RI-1] (introduced 3/9/2007) Cosponsors (274)Became Public Law No: 110-343 [GPO: Text 110-374 Part 1, 110-374 Part 2, 110-374 Part 3Emergency Economic Stabilization Act of 2008. H.R.1424 is the vehicle for the economic rescue legislation. Division A is the Emergency Economic Stabilization Act of 2008; Division B is the Energy Improvement and Extension Act of 2008; and Division C is the Tax Extenders and Alternative Minimum Tax Relief Act of 2008.
