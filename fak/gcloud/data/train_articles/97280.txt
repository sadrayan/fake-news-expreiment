Prime Minister of Canada
Public events for Prime Minister Justin Trudeau, for Wednesday, November 4, 2015:Ottawa 1:30 p.m. The Prime Minister hosts a Google Hangout with schoolchildren immediately after his swearing-in ceremony. It can be accessed at: youtube.com/user/pmocpm. The public and media will be invited to follow the conversation online. During this Google Hangout, the Prime Minister will converse with schoolchildren from five schools across Canada in a bilingual session.
