Korea. South Korea. Incheon. 30 km from Seoul. Family eating dog meat on a saturday lunch in a restaurant specialised with dog food. Incheon. 30 km from Seoul. "Bosintang" dish is made of dog meat cooked in a soup with various spices (ginseng, soya,..) and vegetables. On the side, customers will have a choice of diverse plates with pickles, carots, cabbages,... Dog meat is a delicatessen of korean traditionnal cooking. © 2002 Didier Ruef
