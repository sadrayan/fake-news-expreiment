Watch the 2018 Race for Illinois Governor Forum in Its Entirety
Gubernatorial candidates Bruce Rauner and Sam McCann get heated in the first televised forum of the campaign.

The four candidates for Illinois governor -- Republican Gov. Bruce Rauner, Democratic challenger J.B. Pritzker, Libertarian candidate Kash Jackson and Conservative candidate Sam McCann -- faced off in the first televised forum of the election season on NBC 5 and Telemundo Chicago.
