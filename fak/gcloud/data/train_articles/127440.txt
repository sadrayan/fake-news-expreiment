IBSSA PROMOTION

During an international meeting of the IBSSA held in Tel Aviv, Israel on March 21st, Karl de la Guerra was elected to the position of IBSSA Chairman of the Board, responsible for all security operations internationally involving the IBSSA.

As a result of this promotion, Jonathan Wallace II will assume Karl's current position as IBSSA Representative to North America. Karl will continue to lead the IBSSA International Anti-Human Trafficking

Project.

KDI would like to extend a special thank you to Sir George Popper, President of the IBSSA for nominating Karl to this executive position.
