PHOTOS: Baby found dead in UNIMAID female toilet
Graphic photos of a newborn lying dead in a toilet at a female hostel has joined the numerous such photos that urge people to wonder how callous mankind can be.

According to Supreme Peter who published the photos, the lifeless newborn was found in the female toilet of the Murtala Hostel at the University of Maiduguri, Borno State.

The eyewitness said the owner of the baby tried to force it down into the toilet on Wednesday. One of the photos showed a lady holding what seemed to be the baby retrieved from the toilet while others followed.
