NJToday Summer Edition: August 11, 2011
Today on NJToday Summer Edition, Governor Chris Christie weighs in on the bridge and tunnel toll hikes, and makes a visit down the shore again on day two of a three-day beach walk. Also, debate over whether a New Jersey student should face criminal charges for a lunch room brawl, and New Jersey trounces New York in the battle for the regional little league title. Plus, on NJ Up Close, coverage of the doctor shortage in New Jersey.
