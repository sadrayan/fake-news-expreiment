Search terms for NHS Dialog

NHS Dialog is a portal that provides access to databases such as AMED, CINHAL, EMBASE, and MEDLINE. All the above terms were searched in the same way in the NHS Dialog portal, apart from the truncation symbol *, that has been replaced by $. In order to limit truncation and avoid the possibility of overflow, a number of characters after the wildcard was also specified.
