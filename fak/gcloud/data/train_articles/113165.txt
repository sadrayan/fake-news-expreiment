Palisades Charter Schools Foundation
This is the Palisades Charter Schools Foundation home page. We are a non-profit organization composed primarily of parents, teachers, dedicated community members. Our mission statement: To foster educational innovation and maintain the highest standards of educational excellence within the eight schools of the K-12 Palisades Charter Schools. We were founded in 1997 in association with the Los Angeles Unified School District with the support of the communities of Pacific Palisades and Brentwood, California. Inside you will find descriptions, news, information, photos, and calendars for all of our eight exceptional schools. Within our site you will find informative pages on Palisades, Canyon, Marquez, and Topanga Elementary schools. Furthermore, you will find information on Palisades and Temescal High as well as Paul Revere Middle School. /
