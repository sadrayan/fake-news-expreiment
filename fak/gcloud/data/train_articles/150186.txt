leptocephalus noun

plural leptocephali \ ˌlep-​tə-​ˈse-​fə-​ˌlī , -​ˌlē \

Definition of leptocephalus : a long thin small-headed transparent pelagic first larva of various eels

First Known Use of leptocephalus 1769, in the meaning defined above

History and Etymology for leptocephalus New Latin, from Greek leptos + kephalē head — more at cephalic
