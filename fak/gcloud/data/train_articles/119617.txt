Dr. David Adamson
World-renowned, board certified fertility expert and pioneer David Adamson, MD, founded Advanced Reproductive Care™, Inc. (ARC®) in 1997. Dr. Adamson is a Clinical Professor, ACF, at Stanford University School of Medicine and recognized as one of the country’s top doctors for women. He is past president of the American Society for Reproductive Medicine (ASRM) and a former board member of National Resolve, the leading nonprofit fertility organization for patients. Board certified in reproductive endocrinology and an internationally sought-after lecturer in reproductive medicine and surgery, Dr. Adamson remains connected to the latest innovations in care by seeing patients several days every week.
