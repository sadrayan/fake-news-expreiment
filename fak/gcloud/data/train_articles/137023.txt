World bank set to established Central Bank of Biafra in Abia State
According to Biafra Newspaper, that the World Bank, are fully set to established a Central Bank of Biafra(CBB), which will be located in the capital city of Abia State.

These initiative was established, after series of meeting with the United Nations and the leader of the Indigenous Peoples of Biafra(IPOB), Mazi Nnamdi Kanu, alongside with his team in Switzerland.
