Donate by Text
With a simple text message, you can make a $10 donation to Susan G. Komen for the Cure® by simply texting the word “KOMEN” to 90999. Each $10 donation will be added to your next mobile phone bill.* You can donate the $10.00 amount up to 5 (five) times per monthly billing cycle.



$10 Text Donation Terms and Conditions



*$10.00 donation to Susan G. Komen for the Cure®. Charges will appear on your wireless bill, or be deducted from your prepaid balance. All purchases must be authorized by account holder.
