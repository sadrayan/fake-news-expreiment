Andreas Haeberlen Associate Professor

Computer and Information Science (CIS)

Email | Personal Webpage



Honors and Awards: NSF CAREER Award - 2011, Max Planck Society Otto Hahn Medal - 2010



Research Expertise: Distributed Systems | Computer Networks | Security

Andreas is working on distributed systems, networking, and security. He is particularly interested in large-scale distributed systems that span multiple administrative domains, such as cloud computing platforms or the Internet's interdomain routing system. Recently he has been working on accountability for distributed systems and on Internet access networks.
