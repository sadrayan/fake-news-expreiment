Biographical Information
KELLY, Mike, (1948 - )

Image courtesy of the Member KELLY, Mike, a Representative from Pennsylvania; born in Pittsburgh, Allegheny County, Pa., May 10, 1948; B.A., Notre Dame University, South Bend, Ind., 1970; business owner; member of the Butler Pa., area school board, 1992-1995; member of the Butler County, Pa., council, 2006-2009; elected as a Republican to the One Hundred Twelfth and to the four succeeding Congresses (January 3, 2011-present).
