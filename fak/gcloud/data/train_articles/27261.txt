Rick Pearson speaks with former Chicago Police Superintendent and current Mayoral Candidate, Garry McCarthy, as he shares details about his candidacy and what his primary concerns are with the city that need to be corrected. Garry and Rick discuss the Anti-Violence protest and how government officials and police officers handled everything; what is needed in the West and South areas of the city to lead to overall improvement; and more.
