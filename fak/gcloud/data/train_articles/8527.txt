Office of the Clerk of the U.S. House of Representatives Library
As the official repository of House-produced documents, the House Library is the holder of print volumes dating back to the beginning of Congress. The following documents detailing activity of individual Congresses are available as PDFs.

Final House Calendar

The Final House Calendar provides a detailed summary of all legislative activity conducted by the House of Representatives.

Résumé of Congressional Activity

The Résumé of Congressional Activity provides a comprehensive summation of all legislative business transacted by the House and Senate.
