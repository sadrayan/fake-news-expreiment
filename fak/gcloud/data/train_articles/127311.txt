Funeral arrangements announced for Chris Hixon and Aaron Feis, school shooting heroes
Funeral arrangements have been announced for Chris Hixon, 49, and Aaron Feis, 37, the Marjory Douglas Stoneham High School athletic director and its assistant football coach, who were killed in Wednesday’s massacre as they patrolled the halls.

Visitation for Hixon, who was also the school’s wrestling coach, will be from 3 to 9 p.m., Tuesday at Landmark Funeral Home, 4200 Hollywood Blvd. A Mass of Christian Burial will be at 11 a.m., Wednesday at Nativity Catholic Church, 5220 Johnson Street.

Hixon, who served in Operation Desert Shield and Operation Desert Storm, will be buried with full military honors. Accounts of the shooting have heralded his role as one of the heroes in the tragedy, dying as he tried to protect students.

Visitation for Feis will be from 5 to 9 p.m., Tuesday and Wednesday, at Church by the Glades, 400 Lakeview Drive, Coral Springs. A funeral service will be held at 11 a.m. Thursday at Church by the Glades.

Feis, who also served as a security guard at the school, has been heralded as a hero for his efforts to protect students during the shootings.
