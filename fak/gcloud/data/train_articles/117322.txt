Live Interactive Dinosaur Exhibit & Event for Kids
Discover the Dinosaurs is a unique exhibit consisting of life-like anatomical moving dinosaurs. See favorites like T-Rex, Velociraptor, Triceratops and more! Learn about these mysterious creatures of the past by hands on exhibits like prehistoric poop, Dino Dig and a computerized Bone Scanner. Discover the fun of prehistoric proportions!

*Purchasing a ticket to the Discover the Dinosaurs exhibit is NOT a ticket to enter the fair. An additional FAIR ticket must be purchased to enter the Fair grounds.*
