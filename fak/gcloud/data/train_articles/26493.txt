Odysseas Papadimitriou has more than 15 years of experience in the personal finance industry, spending nearly eight years at Capital One before founding the credit card comparison website CardHub and, later, WalletHub. Mr. Papadimitriou held various positions at Capital One, including senior marketing director, and his many accomplishments include managing a $550 million P&L; and spearheading the largest customer giveback in company history.

Given his experience on both the corporate and consumer sides of the market, Mr. Papadimitriou has become a highly sought-after personal finance expert, whose views are regularly quoted by leading publications, including The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal and The Washington Post. Mr. Papadimitriou graduated Magna Cum Laude from Brown University with a B.A. in Economics and a B.Sc. in Civil Engineering. He holds an MBA from the Fuqua School of Business at Duke University.
