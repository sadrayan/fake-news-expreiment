Senate Framework for Education Reform

Accountability: Reduce number of STAAR tests needed for graduation; allow local districts to determine if end-of-course exam scores count in a student’s final grade.

Choice: Allow students to transfer to any public school in any district where there is room available; create a private school scholarship program paid for through business tax credits.

Charter Schools: Lift the cap that currently limits the number of charter schools at 215; close down existing low-performing charter schools.

Career and Technology: Give students more flexibility to pursue career and technology courses.

Technology: Ensure school districts have needed technology to provide online courses.
