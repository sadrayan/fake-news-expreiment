Beau Biden Funeral
Vice President Joe Biden kisses his granddaughter Natalie before a funeral for her father, former Delaware Attorney General Beau Biden, Saturday, June 6, 2015, at St. Anthony of Padua Roman Catholic Church in Wilmington, Del. Standing with the vice president are his grandson Hunter, from bottom left, son Hunter, daughter-in-law Hallie and wife Jill. Beau Biden, the eldest son of the vice president, died of brain cancer May 30 at age 46. (AP Photo/Patrick Semansky)
