Wits University
School of Accountancy

The Wits School of Accountancy combines the highest standard of academic excellence with preparation for the real world of work. We consistently produce students who are placed in the top ten performers in the national SAICA examinations and are the leading contributor of black accountants to the South African business landscape.

We aim to be an outstanding school of choice for the education of undergraduate and postgraduate students offering quality teaching grounded in relevant research and scholarly activities.

Our mission is to research, conduct scholarly activities, facilitate learning and teach the practice of accountancy in all disciplines in order to ensure that our graduates are employable and bring the highest levels of professional standards as future leaders and life-long learners in professional life.
