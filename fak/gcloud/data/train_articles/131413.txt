Paducah Woman dies in cinema while watching IT remake – Sunday Post
A 41-year-old woman died in a Paducah cinema auditorium Saturday while watching a screening of Stephen Kings horror movie remake, IT.

The incident occurred at the Cinemark Cinema in Paducah, Kentucky. The cinema-goer attended the new release screening with friends and had complained of chest pains and difficulty breathing during one the film’s climax scenes. According to witnesses, the woman let out a large scream and then collapsed in her seat.

The woman’s friends called EMTs however by the time they arrived the woman was unresponsive and died at the scene.
