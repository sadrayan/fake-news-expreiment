N.J. Governor Addresses PA Delegation Breakfast
Following his keynote address on Tuesday night, Governor Chris Christie (R-NJ), along with Sen. Pat Toomey (R-PA) and Sen. Kelly Ayotte (R-NH) delivered remarks at the Pennsylvania State Delegation breakfast in Tampa, Fla.

The Republican Party of Pennsylvania hosts daily breakfasts with delegates, alternates and guests of the Pennsylvania Delegation each morning during the Republican convention.
