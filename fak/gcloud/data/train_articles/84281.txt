CS/CS/SB 392 (2007) - Watershed Restoration [RPCC]
General Bill

Watershed Restoration [RPCC]: provides for expanded uses of bonds issued for Everglades restoration; provides certain requirements before release of funds; creates Caloosahatchee & St. Lucie River Watershed Protection Program; creates St. Lucie River Watershed Protection Plan; expands authorized uses of funds deposited into Save Our Everglades TF; extends period of time in which funds may be deposited into trust fund, etc. Amends Ch. 373, 215.619.

SB Substituted for HB 7157 By Environment & Natural Resources Council and Mayfield
