Clinton Global Initiative
Laureate has partnered with the Clinton Global Initiative (CGI) on a number of initiatives since 2008. CGI convenes global leaders to create and implement innovative solutions to the world's most pressing challenges.

CGI Annual Meeting Laureate supports the Clinton Global Initiative Annual Meetings each year, and broadcasts them to tens of thousands of students and faculty in its network.



CGI University Laureate sponsors and sends student ambassadors to the Clinton Global Initiative University, which challenges college students to address global issues with practical, innovative solutions.
