Hassan Nisar’s mother passes away

LAHORE: The mother of senior columnist Hassan Nisar passed away on Sunday at the age of 94. The deceased was buried at a local graveyard. Maulana Tariq Jameel led her funeral prayers. Leading personalities of the country including President Mamnoon Hussain, Punjab Chief Minister Shahbaz Sharif, PM’s Advisor Irfan Siddiqui, PML-Q President Chaudhry Shujat Hussain, Former CM Chaudhry Pervez Ilahi and Moonis Ilahi have condoled the death.
