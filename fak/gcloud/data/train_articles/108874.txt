Shire Horse
Height: The height for this breed is 21.2 hands. This approximates to 84.8 inches to the withers of the horse. Average height for horse breeds is 15.0 hands or 60 inches. Therefore, the Shire Horse is taller than most horse breeds.

Weight: The Shire Horse weighs 1,800 pounds, which is 775 pounds heavier than the average horse breed.

Life: Most horse breeds tend to live between 25 and 30 years. Not surprisingly, the Shire Horse has a life expectancy in line with other horses.
