Oral Health Maps Oral Health Maps is an interactive mapping application that presents indicators of oral health and preventive interventions for oral health. This application is produced through collaboration among CDC, the Association of State and Territorial Dental Directors, and the participating states. In Oral Health Maps, you can View national and state maps that show indicators for water fluoridation at the state and county level

View national maps that show state-level indicators for untreated tooth decay, caries experience, and dental sealants

View a listing of all the indicators available for a state or county

Create a printer-friendly version of the maps displayed for saving or printing
