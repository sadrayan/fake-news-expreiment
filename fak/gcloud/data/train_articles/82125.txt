18 U.S. Code Chapter 44 - FIREARMS
Tracing of Firearms in Connection With Criminal Investigations

Memorandum of President of the United States, , 78 F.R. 4301, which requires Federal law enforcement agencies to ensure that all firearms recovered after , in the course of criminal investigations and taken into Federal custody are traced through the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives at the earliest time practicable, was editorially reclassified and is set out as a note under section 40901 of Title 34, Crime Control and Law Enforcement.
