Dear Mr. Selby:

On June 26, 2014, the Office of the Governor (the “OOG”) received your request under the Public Information Act for the following information:

I seek access to any texts, emails, letters or other documented evidence of responses to the attached letters from any federal officials including employees of the Department of Homeland Security and the White House up to and including President Barack Obama.

After reviewing our records, we have determined that the OOG has no information responsive to your request. If you have any questions or need additional information, you may contact me at 512-463‑1788.

Sincerely,

Chris Sterner

Assistant General Counsel
