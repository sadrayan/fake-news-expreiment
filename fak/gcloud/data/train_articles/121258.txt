The Conservative Political Action Conference began this morning in Washington, D.C., and will continue through Saturday, March 8.

Slated speakers include former Gov. Sarah Palin, Rep. Paul Ryan, New Jersey Gov. Chris Christie, Louisiana Gov. Bobby Jindal, Sens. Rand Paul, Ted Cruz, and Marco Rubio, and former Pennsylvania Rep. Rick Santorum.
