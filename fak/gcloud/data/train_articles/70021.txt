Posts address the legal issues arising from the legalization of marijuana in Colorado and other states.

Author: Douglas A. Berman is a law professor at The Ohio State University, who also authors Sentencing Law and Policy and is the co-author of a casebook, Sentencing Law and Policy: Cases, Statutes and Guidelines and is a managing editor of the Federal Sentencing Reporter. Other contributors include Robert Mikos, a law professor at Vanderbilt University; and Alex Kreit, a professor at Thomas Jefferson School of Law.
