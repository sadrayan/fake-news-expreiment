Quarterly Reports
Pursuant to MCC â€“ Chpt. 2-78-150(a), COPA is required to post on its website for public review and file with the Mayor or his designee, the Superintendent of the Chicago Police Department, the Chairman of the City Council Committee on Public Safety, and the office of the City Clerk, a quarterly report no later than the fifteenth day of April, July and October of each year. Each quarterly report contains statistical information including the number of investigations opened, pending, closed, sustained and not sustained since the last report. In order to increase the transparency of the work done by COPA, each quarterly report prepared by COPA is published here on our website.
