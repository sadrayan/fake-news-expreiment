Bupyeong Headquarters

GM Koreaâ€™s Bupyeong headquarters includes manufacturing facilities as well as a design center and manufacturing engineering center.

The Bupyeong plant was the first modern vehicle manufacturing facility in Korea. It added an engine shop in 1971 and became the seventh plant to build vehicles with diesel engines in 1985. In 1986, the plant began producing the Lemans global car and became the first automotive facility in Asia to receive ISO 9001 certification.

The plant features two manufacturing facilities that build the Aveo, Trax and Malibu, an engine shop and a KD (knockdown vehicle) plant.
