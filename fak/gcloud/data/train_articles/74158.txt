Collier Resources Company is a minerals management company jointly owned by Barron Collier Companies and Collier Enterprises, the two Collier family owned businesses tracing their roots to Barron Gift Collier, the founder of Collier County. We are part of a 69 year history of oil exploration and production in Southwest Florida and we take pride in our legacy of environmental stewardship.
