DRUG ABUSE PREVENTION AND CONTROL View Entire Chapter F.S. 893.03 893.03 Standards and schedules. — The substances enumerated in this section are controlled by this chapter. The controlled substances listed or to be listed in Schedules I, II, III, IV, and V are included by whatever official, common, usual, chemical, trade name, or class designated.
