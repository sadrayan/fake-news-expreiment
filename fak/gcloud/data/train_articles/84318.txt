CS/SB 1588 (2008) - Property Taxation [FPCC]
General Bill

Property Taxation [FPCC]: Revises the requirements specifying the information that must be included on the real property assessment roll and on the tangible personal property roll. Revises provisions governing the manner in which homestead property may be assessed at less than just value. Authorizes a taxpayer to file an action in circuit court requiring a property appraiser to provide certain information, etc.

SB Substituted for HB 7131 By Policy & Budget Council and Sansom
