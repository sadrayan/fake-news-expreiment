First European State declares Pakistan completely safe for tourist and business travel
ISLAMABAD - In a new development, European state Portugal has become the first one to declare Pakistan completely safe for tourist travel.

The law and order situation in Pakistan has improved significantly over time. Portuguese government has acknowledged the fact by allowing its citizens to tour Pakistan.
