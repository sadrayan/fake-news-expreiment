Dragonsoop
Dragon Soop is something totally new in a can!

A fermented alcoholic beverage fortified with Schnapps, Dragon Soop is 8% abv and contains high levels of caffeine (35mg per 100ml), blended with taurine, guarana and delicious flavours. Dragon Soop comes in nine flavours - Sour Apple, Blue Raspberry, Lemon Sherbet, Red Kola, Strawberry & Lime, Passion Fruit & Orange, Dark Fruit Punch, Tropical Fruit Punch and now NEW Venom.

Always drink in moderation and remember it is not suitable for persons sensitive to caffeine.
