White House Schedule - June 6, 2011
In the morning, the President and the Vice President will receive the Presidential Daily Briefing in the Oval Office. This meeting is closed press. Later in the morning, the President will meet with his national security team for his monthly meeting on Afghanistan and Pakistan in the Situation Room. The Vice President will also attend. This meeting is closed press. In the afternoon, the President will be interviewed by Hearst DC Bureau, WEWS Cleveland and WDIV Detroit about the importance of the auto industry resurgence. These interviews will be in the Eisenhower Executive Office Building and are closed press. Later in the afternoon, the President will meet with senior advisors in the Oval Office. This meeting is closed press.
