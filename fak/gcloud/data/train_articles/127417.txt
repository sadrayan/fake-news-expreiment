Alaska Department of Revenue
Sales and Use Tax



The State of Alaska currently does not have a sales and use tax; however, some local jurisdictions impose local sales taxes.For more information on local taxes, each year the Alaska Department of Commerce, Community and Economic Development publishes Alaska Taxable , an overview of each community, whether it collects taxes, and if it does, its tax rates.is on the website of the department’s Division of Community and Regional Affairs . On the right, under the heading, “Division Programs,” click on “Alaska Taxable Database.”
