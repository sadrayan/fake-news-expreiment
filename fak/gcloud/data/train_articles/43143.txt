Mark Stencel's Summary

Grizzled online news editor and newsroom leader with a couple of decades of digital scar tissue to show for it. Co-author of "The Goat Must Be Fed" for Duke Reporters' Lab (http://www.goatmustbefed.com) and Digital Fellow at the Poynter Institute. Specialties: digital / online journalism; digital media and platforms, from social to mobile; media / newsroom management, consulting and training; publishing and broadcasting; media / content partnerships; labor relations; audience strategy and content strategy (same thing, by the way). Covered U.S. politics and government, science and technology. You _can_ lead journalists to readers -- and make them link.
