The following numbers are derived from the U.S. Census Bureau's resident population estimates from April 1, 2010 to July 1, 2013.

Austin-Round Rock, TX Metro Area

Between April 1, 2010 and July 1, 2013 there was an estimated average of 141 new persons per day. Of these, about 93 (66.8%) were estimated to be net migrants.

Travis County, Texas

Between April 1, 2010 and July 1, 2013 there was an estimated average of 82 new persons per day. Of these, about 50 (61.4%) were estimated to be net migrants.

Austin city, Texas
