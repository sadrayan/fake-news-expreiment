Sabrina Carpenter Covers Girls’ Life Magazine
Sabrina Carpenter is on the cover of Girls’ Life magazine. The actress is on the cover of the magazine’s December/January issue.

Sabrina released the cover via her social media pages today, November 20. Sabrina is currently on break from her hit Disney Channel show, Girl Meets World. However, new episodes of the show are still airing and the next one will air on December 4.
