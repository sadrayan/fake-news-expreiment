Abortions
Wisconsin Medicaid and BadgerCare Plus cover abortions in the following situations:

If an abortion is medically necessary to save the life of the woman.

If continuing the pregnancy will cause grave and long-lasting damage to the physical health of the woman.

If the pregnancy was the result of a sexual assault or incest.

A doctor must sign a written statement attesting to one of the above situations.

Wisconsin Medicaid pays for office visits and other related services for a covered abortion.
