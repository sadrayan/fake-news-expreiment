International Trade and Investment Country Facts
In 2016, exports to Canada accounted for 14.5% of total U.S. exports, and imports from Canada accounted for 11.6% of total U.S. imports. Exports were led by industrial supplies and materials, which accounted for 22.1% of U.S. exports to Canada, and imports were led by industrial supplies and materials, which accounted for 38.8% of U.S. imports from Canada.
