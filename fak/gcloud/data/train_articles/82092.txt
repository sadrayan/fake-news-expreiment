Most Feared Plaintiffs Firm: Cohen Milstein
Law360, New York (November 5, 2014, 4:00 PM EST) -- Cohen Milstein Sellers & Toll PLLC helped consumers reach an unusually structured $450 million settlement with Apple Inc. earlier this year in a hotly contested and closely watched antitrust suit over the company’s marketing of e-books, leading the firm to repeat as one of Law360’s most feared plaintiffs firms. Cohen Milstein was one of 10 firms named to Law360’s 2014 Feared Plaintiffs roster after it made the inaugural list last year, but managing partner Steven J. Toll told Law360 that fear isn’t the sense the firm’s attorneys seek to instill in the opposition. “I look at it as 'respected,'” Toll said....
