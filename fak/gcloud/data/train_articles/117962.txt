About EPN Europhysics News is the magazine of the European physics community. It is owned by the European Physical Society and produced in cooperation with EDP Sciences. It is distributed to all our Individual Members and many institutional subscribers. Most EPS Member Societies receive EPN for further distribution to their members. The total circulation is currently about 25000 copies per issue. Editor: V.R. Velasco
