Shutdowns: Sara Lee Corp. said Wednesday that it will close three Champion Products factories by the end of the year. The company said the Norwich, N.Y., plant will be closed by Nov. 1 after 80 years of operation. Two other plants, in Midway, Ga., and Slocomb, Ala., are also scheduled to close this year. The Norwich plant employs 230 people, all of whom will be offered severance pay and benefits packages as well as job replacement and retraining assistance from the company, said Jack Ward, senior vice president of Sara Lee.
