Rick Perry timeline
1950

Born in Haskell.

1972

Graduated from Texas A&M University.

1977

Discharged from U.S. Air Force with rank of captain.

1984

Elected to the Texas House of Representatives.

1989

Switched from the Democratic Party, in which he had a conservative voting record, to the Republican Party.

1990

Elected Texas agriculture commissioner.

1998

Elected lieutenant governor.

2000

Became governor when Gov. George W. Bush became president.
