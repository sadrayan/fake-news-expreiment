Taxes — Barack Obama
Asking millionaires to pay their fair share No household making more than $1 million each year should pay a smaller share of their income in taxes than a middle-class family pays.

Investing in the middle class President Obama knows we can’t simply cut our way to prosperity. His budget includes investments in education, manufacturing, and infrastructure, while bringing discretionary spending to its lowest level as a share of the economy in more than 50 years .
