Suozzi's in the money Nassau legislature approves pay hikes for executive, clerk, comptroller, assessor and DA effective Jan. 1

Abstract (Document Summary)

Nassau County Executive Thomas Suozzi is set to get a 60 percent raise Jan. 1, following a vote by the county legislature yesterday to approve pay increases for the five officials who are elected countywide.
