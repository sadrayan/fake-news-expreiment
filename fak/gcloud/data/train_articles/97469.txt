Barcroft Studios
Established by CEO Sam Barcroft in London in 2003, we're dedicated to creating world-class television and digital video content celebrating a diverse range of incredible true stories. We create engaging, inspiring and popular documentary shows for global platforms and broadcasters around the world.

Our channel Barcroft TV is one of the biggest factual brands on the internet and is followed by millions of people across YouTube, Snapchat and Facebook Watch.

We also work with brand partners to create and distribute authentic content that gets people talking. From NGOs to sportswear companies, automotive manufacturers to telecoms providers, we deliver compelling stories that resonate with their brand and get audiences talking.
