The Daily News' original coverage of 'War of the Worlds'
The "space cartridge" was supposed to have struck at Grover's Mills, an actual town near Princeton. Names of well-known highways were used in describing the advance of the monsters. The voice had warned them to "pack up and move north because the machines are coming from Mars." The dramatization of Wells' novel had featured a fictitious speech from "The Governor of New Jersey," assuring the public that the National Guard has been mobilized to fight the "Martian monsters" and the Harlem residents had confused the mythical "Governor" with the President.
