Cardiff Gaza march disorder: Eight men sentenced
Video

Seven men involved in a major disturbance as thousands of campaigners marched against the Gaza conflict in Cardiff have been jailed.

Nicholas Carter, 32, John James Williams, 32, Ashan Malik, 57, Daniel Woods, 23, Barry Murphy, 34, Yussef Asad, 28, and Grant Ashcroft, 23, were sentenced for violent disorder.

Daniel Smout, 25, of Shropshire, previously admitted affray. All but Malik received custodial sentences - which totalled 10-and-a-half years - at Cardiff Crown Court on Wednesday.

Footage of the disorder was captured and later posted on YouTube.
