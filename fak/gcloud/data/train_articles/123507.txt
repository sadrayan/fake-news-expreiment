About Us – Phembani
Before our acquisition of Shanduka in 2015, Phembani had investments in different aspects of the energy, resources and infrastructure related sectors including:

Oil and gas: Engen and Afric Oil;

Engen and Afric Oil; Coal: Exxaro, BECSA, and Phembani Coal Carolina; and

Exxaro, BECSA, and Phembani Coal Carolina; and Infrastructure: AfriSam

With a focus on longer term investments and building partnerships with fellow shareholders, Phembani prefers to be an active investor, and in some cases, has operational control of its investments.

Based in South Africa, Phembani’s investment focus is on taking advantage of the key growth markets in Africa.
