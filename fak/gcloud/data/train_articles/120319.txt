Les œuvres d’art contemporaines de Laurence Jenkell
The twist and spiral of my candy sculpture gives all its expression and dynamism to my artwork, and also makes each candy sculpture unique. This is the reason why I worked on a new artwork, based on the twist of the candy. By analysing my candy sculpture, by isolating its skeleton, its papillotte, the twist, the core and by stacking several spirals, the double helix and thus the molecule of heredity became imperative upon me:

The DNA would be my new artwork direction.
