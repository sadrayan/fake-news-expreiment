Meeting Agendas & Minutes – City of Buckeye, Arizona
Let’s Get Together

The City of Buckeye holds regular meetings on the 1st and 3rd Tuesdays of each month at 6:00pm—we strongly encourage the public to attend.

The agendas for each meeting are posted beforehand, in the locations listed below. We also post the minutes from the meeting, just in case you couldn’t make it and want to find out what you missed. The minutes from each City Council meeting are approved by the City Council at their next regularly scheduled meeting, and then posted at that time.

Posting Locations

City Hall, 530 East Monroe Avenue, Buckeye, Arizona 85326

The City’s Official Website: www.buckeyeaz.gov
