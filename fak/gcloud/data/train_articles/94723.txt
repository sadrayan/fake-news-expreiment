Conservatives Rank Candidates Ahead of Election Day
The state conservative party already picked its candidates for this year's legislative races, but now it's giving voters more information about which lawmakers are the most conservative. The party released its legislative rankings this week. Assemblyman Dean Murray, a Long Island Republican, topped the list with a score of 95%, with Senators Fred Akshar, of Binghamton, and Simcha Felder, a Brooklyn Democrat who caucuses with the GOP, tied for tops in their chamber at 90%. Chairman Mike Long discusses the scores.
