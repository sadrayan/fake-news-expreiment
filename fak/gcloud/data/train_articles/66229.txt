What a waterboarding reconstruction looks like - BBC News
Published on Aug 3, 2015

**VIDEO CONTAINS SOME DISTURBING IMAGES** The BBC's Panorama programme witnesses the first accurate public demonstration of waterboarding. A team based in the United States showed the BBC Panorama's Hilary Andersson what is involved.

Panorama: Fighting Terror With Torture is on BBC One at 20:30 BST on Monday 3 August 2015 and available later via BBC iPlayer (UK Only).
