Memorial held for dead Northern White Rhino ' Sudan'
Published on Mar 31, 2018

Poachers and those found with game trophies could face the death sentence if a proposal by the tourism ministry comes into force. Speaking during the memorial of Sudan, the last of the male northern white rhino, Tourism Cabinet Secretary Najib Balala said this would help deter poaching in the country.
