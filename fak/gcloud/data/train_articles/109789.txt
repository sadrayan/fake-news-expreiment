'The Dresser' unites Ian McKellen and Anthony Hopkins for the first time
The cast of Starz's first original film, The Dresser, is hardly lacking in star power.

With Ian McKellen and Anthony Hopkins, the film for TV marks the first time the two have joined forces for a project.

SEE ALSO: Starz joins the subscription streaming service club

In the movie, an adaptation of a successful play, stars Hopkins as an aging actor and McKellen as his assistant.

In the piece above, the creative team talks about brining the project from stage to screen and McKellen and Hopkins open up about working with one another for the first time.

The movie premieres May 30 on Starz.
