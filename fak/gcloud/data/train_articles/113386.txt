The JonBenet Ramsey Case
" We are making progress, and we are moving forward"

-- Comdr. Mark Beckner, lead investigator, Dec. 5, 1997

A lmost 2,000 children were murdered in the United States during 1996, but none of those murders has fascinated the American public and attracted the mass media like that of JonBenet Ramsey. And a year after the little girl died, the spotlight is still hot and her killing is still unsolved.
