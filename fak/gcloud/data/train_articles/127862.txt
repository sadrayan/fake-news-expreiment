National Automobile Club
Effective October 1st, the National Automobile Club no longer issues International Driving Permits (IDPs). You may obtain an IDP from the American Automobile Touring Alliance (AATA). If you have any questions in regards to IDPs, please contact the AATA directly.



AATA contact information:





IDP applications received prior to October 1st were processed by the National Automobile Club. Applications received after October 1st will be rerouted to the AATA during the transition period. For questions or comments in regards to previously issued IDPs, please contact the NAC at IDP@thenac.com
