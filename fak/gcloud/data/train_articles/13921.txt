Meet the Team
Brian is a veteran lobbyist who is well-versed in legislative issues and processes. His deep relationships and experience have delivered wins for clients in the areas of education, public safety, workforce development and general business interests.

Brian has been a key advocate in successfully lobbying for the expansion of Pennsylvania’s Educational Improvement Tax Credit program and the interests of charter schools and hybrid learning.

Brian served Govs. Tom Ridge and Mark Schweiker as the director of legislative and public affairs for the Department of Labor and Industry, acting as the primary lobbyist, and he was a member of the political staff of Gov. Ridge’s reelection campaign. Additionally, Brian volunteered for the presidential campaign of George W. Bush. He is a graduate of Western Michigan University.
