In Chicago Thousands March in Solidarity with Palestine today.
Today, Chicago's streets were blocked by Police and watched by onlookers as Americans from all walks of life peacefully protested against the oppression, destruction and massacres happening now in Palestine ~ Gaza.

Many children marched with families in solidarity with their "friends" from Palestine. Thousands showed their passions by holding signs and chanting "Free Free Palestine." as they marched for miles throughout the city.
