Alaska Department of Revenue
Juneau Tax Division Office Mailing Address Update



All mail sent to Tax Division offices in Juneau through the U.S. Postal Service must be addressed to PO Box 110420. Beginning October 1, all mail addressed to our Willoughby Avenue addresses will not be delivered and will be returned to sender by the Post Office. This change does not impact Anchorage Tax Division offices.



Tax Division Office Closure



The Tax Division offices will be closed on Friday, October 19 for a mandatory furlough day and will reopen Monday, October 22. Revenue Online will remain available to conduct business. Should you need assistance during this time, please call the mainline of the respective office location: Anchorage at 907. 269-6620 or Juneau at 907.465.2320, and leave a message.
