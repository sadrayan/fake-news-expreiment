KNOXVILLE (WATE) - Simon Malls says its two Knoxville shopping malls will open at 6 p.m. on Thanksgiving night.

West Town Mall and Knoxville Center Mall will then close a 1 a.m. Friday morning, reopening at 6 a.m. on Black Friday. The malls will then close at 10 p.m. that evening.

Select stores will be open throughout Thanksgiving night and into Black Friday.

The malls will also extend hours over Thanksgiving weekend, opening from 8 a.m. to 10 p.m. on Saturday, November 29, and from 10 a.m. to 8 p.m. on Sunday, November 30.

Leading up to Christmas, the malls will open Wednesday, December 24 from 7 a.m. to 6 p.m. They will close on Christmas Day and reopen December 26 from 6 a.m. to 10 p.m.
