Newsmakers Governor Lincoln Chafee
EAST PROVIDENCE, R.I. (WPRI) - This week on Newsmakers: Gov. Lincoln Chafee. With the surprise announcement that he was not going to run for reelection in 2014, Governor Chafee talks about his priorities for the last 16 months of his administration.

Chafee also responds to questions raised about the lack of oversight of the 38 Studios deal and provides updates on the still non-operational computer system at the DMV.

The governor also talks about his call for a constitutional convention.
