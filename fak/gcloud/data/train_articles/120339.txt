Part 3, Section 13, 27 November 1956
Archival Creator: Kennedy, John F. (John Fitzgerald), 1917-1963

Series Description: "Strategy of Peace" is a compilation of John F. Kennedy's statements on issues thought to be important in the 1960 Presidential Campaign, published in that year. This series includes manuscript materials and mimeograph drafts. Correspondence, galley proofs and other data may be found in the President's Office Files, Series 10. Personal Secretary's Files and also in Series 11. Special Events Through the Years.

General Records Type: Textual Records
