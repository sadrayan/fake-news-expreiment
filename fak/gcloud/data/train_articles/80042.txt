About IDEA Public Schools



IDEA Public Schools is a growing network of tuition-free K-12 public charter schools serving more than 20,000 students in 36 schools throughout the Rio Grande Valley, Austin and San Antonio. IDEA is committed to "College For All Children" and has sent 100% of its graduates to college for seven years.



Mission

IDEA Public Schools prepares students from underserved communities for success in college and citizenship.
