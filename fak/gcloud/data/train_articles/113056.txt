Necrotizing Fasciitis
Here you will learn about a quick-killing bacterial disorder which is referred to in the tabloids as the Flesh-Eating Disease. A rare variant of strep, it rots the flesh right off ya'. Reading about this disease is not for the faint of heart, nor for those paranoid types who are liable to think every sore throat they get for the next 20 years is in fact, the prelude to Necrotizing Fasciitis.
