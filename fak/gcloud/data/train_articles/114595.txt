Pierre Trudeau with his Bride Margaret
Canada’s Prime Minister Pierre Trudeau (foreground) leans over to kiss an unidentified young lady to the seeming surprise of his recent bride Margaret, left, in St. Joseph Du Lac, Quebec March 27, 1971. Prime Minister and Mrs. Trudeau spent Saturday at maple free farm here near Montreal at a sugaring out party for Mr. Trudeau’s constituents of Mount-Royal. (AP Photo/PBR)
