Food-Info.net> E-numbers > E900-1000 E900 : Dimethyl-polysiloxane Origin:

Synthetic polymer. Function & Characteristics:

Used as anti-foaming agent and as a protective layer on fats and oils against oxygen. Products:

Industrial oils, soups, juices Acceptable daily intake (ADI) :

Up to 1,5 mg/kg bodyweight Side effects:

None known Dietary restrictions:

None. E900 can be used by all religious groups, vegetarians and vegans.
