In St. Petersburg, Johnny Depp’s car crashed into the wall
American actor and musician Johnny Depp got into an accident in St. Petersburg. It is reported “The Fifth Channel” .

As noted, the incident occurred on Voznesensky Prospekt, when Depp’s car left the inner courtyard of the Four Seasons Hotel. The driver did not fit into the turn and crashed into the wall.

Previously Johnny Depp personally met with several dozen fans during his visit to Moscow . A total of 40 girls paid 62 thousand rubles. for an audience with the star of “Pirates of the Caribbean”, who came to Russia as part of the rock band Hollywood Vampires. According to the Russians who met with the actor, they had to stand in line and wait for everyone to be summoned to the room to Depp.
