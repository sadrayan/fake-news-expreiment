‘Back to the Future’: Why October 21, 2015, Is the Day Marty McFly Arrived in the Future
In “Back to the Future Part II,” Marty McFly (Michael J. Fox) touched down in Hill Valley, Calif., on October 21, 2015 after blasting through the space-time continuum with Doc Brown (Christopher Lloyd). For “Back to the Future” fans, today’s date has been a revered pop-culture milestone. That futuristic look 30 years into the future, is now the present.
