Lady Gaga calls Melania Trump's anti-bullying stance 'hypocrisy'

Skip in Skip x Embed x Share Lady Gaga made it clear where she stands in this election. USA TODAY

Lady Gaga has a Million Reasons she's not pleased with Melania Trump.

The wife of GOP presidential candidate Donald Trump gave a speech explaining that she would work to end cyber-bullying if she becomes first lady, and the Joanne singer took issue with that initiative.

Gaga tweeted at Melania Sunday, calling her a hypocrite for proposing to fight online bullying while her husband is "(one) of the most notorious bullies we have ever witnessed."

Lady Gaga and Melania Trump (Photo: AP)

.@MELANIATRUMP to say u will stand for "anti-bullying" is hypocrisy. Your husband is 1 of the most notorious bullies we have ever witnessed. — #VoteHillary (@ladygaga) November 6, 2016

The star then urged her followers to vote for Hillary Clinton, to "stop this dangerous man from continuing to divide and wreck our democracy."
