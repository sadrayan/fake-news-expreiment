Sharon Kilzer

Theodore Roosevelt Center Project Manager

Sharon Kilzer, Theodore Roosevelt Center Project Manager, is a Dickinson State University alumna with degrees in business administration and business education. She also holds a master's degree in theology and Christian ministry from Franciscan University of Steubenville, Ohio, and is a certified public accountant.

Bridging the worlds of education and executive administration, Kilzer identifies and coordinates the resources to bring the TR Center vision to reality.
