Keeping Virginia the Best Managed State in the Nation

Virginia is routinely cited as one of the best-governed states in the nation. The efficiency and effectiveness of the Commonwealth’s government is a key factor in maintaining the Commonwealth’s AAA Bond rating, attracting new and expanded business opportunities, providing our citizens with necessary services, and giving taxpayers the best value for their money.

Terry will not allow this competitive advantage to erode. Additional improvements in government efficiency and effectiveness will only happen through innovation and evaluation. As governor, Terry will provide the leadership and focus necessary to move Virginia to the next level. Utilizing the Council on Virginia’s Future as the coordinating body, Terry will maintain the Commonwealth’s management focus overall and drill down on a series of targeted operational efficiency reviews.
