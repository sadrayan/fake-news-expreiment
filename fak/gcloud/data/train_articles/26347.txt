Full Remarks: Virginia Governor Terry McAuliffe Speaks to COG Board about the "New Virginia Economy"
The Governor of the Commonwealth of Virginia, Terry McAuliffe, spoke before the Council of Governments (COG) Board of Directors at their July meeting, outlining his plan for a New Virginia Economy and expressing his support for regional collaboration. Since taking office 17 months ago, McAuliffe has focused on building a “New Virginia Economy.”
