NYC immigrant communities shaken after federal raids led to around 40 arrests
"I'm scared. We've always been scared because of our situation, but now it feels different. It feels like they are out looking for us, for everyone who is undocumented," said an undocumented Mexican deliveryman who would only give his name as Cruz. He said he and his wife had talked to their children, who are citizens ages 13 and 18, about the worst-case scenario.
