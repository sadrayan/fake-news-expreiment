The sub-branch of Syrian Arab Red Crescent in Douma was declared out of service
The Syrian Arab Red Crescent (SARC) calls all parties to neutralize humanitarian facilities, aid workers and staff in areas that suffer from the crisis where SARC serves civilians and people in dire need for emergency aid and humanitarian response in the Syrian Arab Republic.

The sub-branch of Syria Arab Red Crescent (SARC) in Douma was declared out of service on April 7th 2018 due to the ongoing situation in Douma. SARC will continue to operate its humanitarian mandate to serve all Syrians across the Country.

The Syrian Arab Red Crescent reiterates the importance to guarantee the safety of its neutral volunteers and humanitarian facilities.
