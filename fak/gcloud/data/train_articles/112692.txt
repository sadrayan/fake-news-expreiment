Front cover of Subterranean Shropshire

Author: Steve Powell

Publisher: Tempus Publishing

ISBN 0-7524-2761-X

This book was published in 2002. It has descriptions, sketches and photos of all manner of underground structures across Shropshire. These include natural caves as well as hand carved grottos and the like. An interesting read for people acquainted with the county.
