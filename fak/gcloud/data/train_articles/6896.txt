Frequently Asked Questions
Who created the sculpture?

Gutzon Borglum and 400 workers.

How much did the sculpture cost?

$989,992.32

How long did it take to build?

14 years - from October 4, 1927 to October 31,1941.

Are the faces eroding?

No, the estimated erosion rate is 1 inch every 10,000 years.

Who is the mountain named after?

Charles E. Rushmore, a New York City attorney, in 1885 who was here on

business.

Were there any deaths during the carving?

No.
