Humalog Prices, Coupons & Savings Tips
Humalog (insulin lispro) is a short-acting type of insulin. Insulin helps to control blood sugar levels in diabetes mellitus, including diabetes type 1 and diabetes type 2. Humalog is more popular than other insulins.

Check our savings tips for co-pay cards, assistance programs, and other ways to reduce your cost.The most common version of Humalog is covered by 85% of insurance plans at a co-pay of $20.00-$45.00, however, some pharmacy coupons or cash prices may be lower.
