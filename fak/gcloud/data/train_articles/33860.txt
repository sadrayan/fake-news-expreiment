Actions - H.R.1911 - 113th Congress (2013-2014): Bipartisan Student Loan Certainty Act of 2013
Chamber All Actions House Presented to President.

House Motion to reconsider laid on the table Agreed to without objection.

House On motion that the House suspend the rules and agree to the Senate amendment Agreed to by the Yeas and Nays: (2/3 required): 392 - 31 (Roll no. 426). (text as House agreed to Senate amendment: CR H5214-5215)

House Considered as unfinished business. (consideration: CR H5240-5241)

House At the conclusion of debate, the Yeas and Nays were demanded and ordered. Pursuant to the provisions of clause 8, rule XX, the chair announced that further proceedings on the motion would be postponed.

House DEBATE - The House proceeded with forty minutes of debate on the motion to agree in the Senate amendment to H.R. 1911.

House Mr. Kline moved that the House suspend the rules and agree to the Senate amendment. (consideration: CR H5214-5221)

House Motion to reconsider laid on the table Agreed to without objection.

House On passage Passed by recorded vote: 221 - 198 (Roll no. 183).

House On motion to recommit with instructions Failed by the Yeas and Nays: 194 - 223 (Roll no. 182).

House The previous question on the motion to recommit with instructions was ordered without objection. (consideration: CR H2938)

House DEBATE - The House proceeded with 10 minutes of debate on the Sinema motion to recommit with instructions pending a reservation of a point of order. The instructions contained in the motion seek to require the bill to be reported back to the House with an amendment adding a new section pertaining to protecting students from teaser interest rates that lead to higher long-term costs. Subsequently, the reservation was removed.

House Ms. Sinema moved to recommit with instructions to Education and the Workforce. (consideration: CR H2937-2939; text: CR H2937)

House The previous question was ordered pursuant to the rule. (consideration: CR H2937)

House DEBATE - The House proceeded with one hour of debate on H.R. 1911.

House Rule provides for consideration of H.R. 1911 with 1 hour of general debate. Previous question shall be considered as ordered without intervening motions except motion to recommit with or without instructions. Measure will be considered read. Bill is closed to amendments.

House Considered under the provisions of rule H. Res. 232. (consideration: CR H2926-2940; text of amendment in the nature of a substitute: CR H2927)

House Rule H. Res. 232 passed House.

House Rules Committee Resolution H. Res. 232 Reported to House. Rule provides for consideration of H.R. 1911 with 1 hour of general debate. Previous question shall be considered as ordered without intervening motions except motion to recommit with or without instructions. Measure will be considered read. Bill is closed to amendments.

House Placed on the Union Calendar, Calendar No. 57.

House Committee on Budget discharged.

House Reported (Amended) by the Committee on Education and the Workforce. H. Rept. 113-82, Part I.

House Ordered to be Reported (Amended) by the Yeas and Nays: 24 - 13.

House Committee Consideration and Mark-up Session Held.

House Referred to the Committee on Education and the Workforce, and in addition to the Committee on the Budget, for a period to be subsequently determined by the Speaker, in each case for consideration of such provisions as fall within the jurisdiction of the committee concerned.
