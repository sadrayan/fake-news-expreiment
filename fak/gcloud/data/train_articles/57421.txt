PHOTOS: Atlanta Rallies On Anniversary of Women’s March
Demonstrators from Los Angeles to New York marched in support of female empowerment and denounced President Donald Trump’s views on immigration, abortion, LGBT rights and women’s rights on Saturday, the anniversary of his inauguration.

In Atlanta women marched in Woodruff Park and gathered at a related Power to the Polls event at the Bakery in southwest Atlanta for a rally that featured speakers such as Stacey Abrams, Alyssa Milano and Women’s March national leaders Linda Sarsour and Tamika Mallory.
