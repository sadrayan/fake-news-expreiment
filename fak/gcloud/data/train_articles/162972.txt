Paper Planes
Premiered May 14, 2019

Playing is an essential part of growing up for children and helps with their development and overall happiness. But for some of the 3.7 million orphans in South Africa, toys and the important part they play in childhood are simply not there. To highlight this, we released thousands of paper planes, encouraging South Africa to help change it.
