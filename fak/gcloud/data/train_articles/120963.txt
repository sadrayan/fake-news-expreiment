Laura Ingraham

Laura is the host of “The Ingraham Angle” on Fox News (weeknights, 10 p.m. ET). She is also host of “The Laura Ingraham Show” and the most listened-to woman in talk radio. She is the author of several New York Times bestselling books, including her latest, “Billionaire at the Barricades,” as well as “Power to the People,” “Shut Up & Sing,” and “The Obama Diaries.” In her previous life, she was a litigator, a Supreme Court law clerk, and a White House staffer and speechwriter. A mother of three young children, she is a passionate supporter of both domestic and international adoption. Twitter: @ingrahamangle
