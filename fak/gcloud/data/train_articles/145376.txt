65 Pumpkin Spice Foods That Have No Business Being Pumpkin Spiced
Nothing spells fall like the return of an alarmingly broad array of pumpkin spice-flavored foods. Despite whispers that the pumpkin spice supply may be exceeding the actual demand for it — and that another seasonal flavor is trying to steal pumpkin spice’s thunder — companies are still rolling out old favorites and new concoctions featuring the ambiguously spiced and vaguely squash-like flavor at an alarming rate. From doggy lattés to applesauce packets and kombucha, here are 65 pumpkin spice-flavored items, currently on the market, that no one asked for.
