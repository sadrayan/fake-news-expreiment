We make above average comedy
Marc Lieberman serves as the Senior Vice President of Business Development, overseeing Above Average’s studio division. He leads the company’s efforts to develop, produce and distribute original programming outside of Above Average’s publishing arm, and has implemented deals with both traditional and digital network partners. Marc came to Above Average from The Onion, where he served as the Vice President of Business Development as well as a producer for the Onion News Network, for which he won a Peabody and numerous Webby awards.

Marc’s first job in the entertainment industry was as an assistant to Bobby Newmyer at Outlaw Productions, working on films such as Training Day and Santa Clause 2. He then went on to cofound Cavalier Films where he produced and financed independent films including Familiar Strangers and Adam. Marc began his career working in corporate finance at Intel and Macromedia.
