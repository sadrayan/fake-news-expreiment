Six staffers injured in Kingman prison altercation
Six staff members at the Arizona State Prison Complex in Kingman were injured Wednesday evening during an inmate altercation.

According to Arizona Department of Corrections spokesperson Andrew Wilder, the altercation occurred around 6 p.m. involving one inmate being pursued by other inmates.

Five of the injured staffers were treated on site, and one was taken to a hospital for further evaluation.

The Department of Corrections has accounted for all inmates and all staff, and they are working to get the inmates involved in the incident locked down for the night, Wilder said.
