Other Group Companies

Manipal Technologies Limited(MTL), based in Manipal was started in 1941 as Manipal Power Press offering print services to Banks. Over the years MTL has grown as end to end secure print service provider offering varied services such as security printing, variable data printing, digital printing, book printing, pre media services, banking cards, smart cards, card management services, outsourced print management services, smart cards, packaging solutions, payment system, examination solutions etc. and is specialised in offering solutions to banking, publishing, government, telecom, retail, pharma, FMCG etc. MTL is now serving customers across the globe and is having units in all metro cities in India, Kenya, Nigeria and Germany.
