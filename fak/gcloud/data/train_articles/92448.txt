Massive American Dream mall tricky for governor candidate Gwen Graham
Asked if a massive development on the edge of the Everglades was a good idea, Graham told WFOR-Ch. 4: “I’m so proud of my family’s long history in South Florida and our commitment to public service. And that project has been something that’s been through all of the proper channels, and will continue, as we’ve always conducted business in South Florida, to go through all of the proper channels.”
