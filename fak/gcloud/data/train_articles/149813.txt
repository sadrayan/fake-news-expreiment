Collection of 6 Vintage Photographs of Jim Morrison, 1967

This is a very rare collection of 6 vintage photographs of Jim Morrison of The Doors. They were taken by Gloria Stavers, then editor, for 16 Magazine in 1967. Each photograph is personally stamped by the Gloria Stavers studio with handwritten notes.

These come from the private collection of the legendary Danny Fields; who signed the MC5, signed and managed Iggy and The Stooges, managed the Ramones and worked in various roles with Jim Morrison, the Velvet Underground and the Modern Lovers.

Dimensions: photo size 7.5 x 5 in.
