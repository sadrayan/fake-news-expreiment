MELTDOWN: CNN Drops Almost 30 Percent
CNN’s ratings are in complete free fall while Fox News’ increased since the same week last year, according to a new report.

AdWeek AdWeek reports that during the week of May 7-12, CNN lost almost 30 percent of its viewers compared to the same time last week.

Fox News, on the other hand, actually gained viewers in comparison to the same time last week.
