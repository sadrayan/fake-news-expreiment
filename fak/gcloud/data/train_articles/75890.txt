Office of Legislative Affairs

The Office of Legislative Affairs is responsible for coordinating the department's lobbying efforts, preparing and reviewing legislative proposals, and informing the legislature and other interested parties about the department's position on legislative issues. The office also serves as a liaison between the department and legislators.
