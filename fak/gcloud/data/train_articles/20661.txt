Presidential Job Approval Center
Presidential job approval is a simple, yet powerful, measure of the public's view of the U.S. president's job performance at a particular point in time.

Beginning in January 2019, Gallup will report Donald Trump's job approval on a monthly basis. Learn more.

Approval ratings for Presidents Barack Obama and Donald Trump are based on weekly Gallup Daily tracking averages. Approval ratings for Presidents Harry Truman through George W. Bush are based on periodic Gallup polls that typically were conducted for four days.

Buy a subscription to our database, Gallup Analytics, to access and export weekly, monthly and yearly presidential job approval ratings.
