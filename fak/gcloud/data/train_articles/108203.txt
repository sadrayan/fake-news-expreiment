Serbia President plants tree in Alley of Honored Guests in Minsk

Serbia President Tomislav Nikolic planted a tree in the Alley of Honored Guests near the Palace of Independence in Minsk on 19 November. Attending the ceremony was Belarus President Alexander Lukashenko.

In the photo: Alexander Lukashenko and Tomislav Nikolic during the planting ceremony.
