March 7: Halim Dhanidina on 'Challenges and Opportunities for American-Muslims Post-9/11'
Los Angeles County Superior Court Judge Halim Dhanidina, the first Muslim judge in California, talks about his experiences on the bench as well as what he sees as the challenges and opportunities facing American Muslims post-9/11. Monday, March 7, 12:30 p.m. at Haines Hall, Room A18. Free and open to the public.

Sponsored by the Center for Near Eastern Studies. Find more information at this UCLA International Institute webpage.
