Governor's Budget Reports
Each year, the Governor recommends to the General Assembly a budget for the State of Georgia. This proposed budget and an amended version of the current year’s budget are detailed in the Governor’s Annual Budget Reports.

PDF versions of the current budget reports are available to download below.

Print copies are available for purchase through More Business Solutions, which has a location in the Sloppy Floyd Building. To order, email statesupport@morebizz.net and include contact information and the number of sets of the budget reports being ordered. Copies of reports may be picked up at the More Business Solutions Center in the Sloppy Floyd Building.

PDFs of previous years’ budgets are available here.

The approved budget is published shortly after the end of the legislative session. For more on this finalized budget, please see Budget in Briefs.
