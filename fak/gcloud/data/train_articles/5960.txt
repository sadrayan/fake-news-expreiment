Newt Gingrich on Climbing Higher in GOP Presidential Polls
6:16 pm ET June 27, 2011

Newt Gingrich wants to “turn this country around” and tells Neil why he is running for president. When asked about the “soap opera” that has been going on with his campaign, Newt answered that “it is very difficult to get past the gossip, but I’m determined to keep campaigning for our country.”

Also, hear why Gingrich “rejects the Obama technique of pretending he is not the president of the United States.”
