Fort Worth ISD
With over 80,000 students in 83 elementary schools, 28 middle schools and 6th grade centers, 14 high schools and 17 special campuses, Fort Worth ISD enjoys a diverse student population and strong community partnerships. Under the leadership of the superintendent and the Board of Education, the District is undergoing a series of initiatives that will redesign, transform and revitalize Fort Worth ISD Schools.
