Federal Courts Web Archive

This page provides access to the Federal Courts Web Archive. The Federal Courts Web Archive provides unique resources for scholars and others conducting retrospective research into the work of the federal judiciary. If you have any questions, please contact us through Ask A Librarian.

*Please use the drop down menu or map to select a state or territory. You can also click on a number on the map to access a United States Circuit Court of Appeal.
