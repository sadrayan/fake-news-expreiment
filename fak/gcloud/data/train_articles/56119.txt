Labor Market Reports
This monthly newsletter contains a lead article on economic issues of importance to our readers. Current employment and unemployment statistics for the state and Metropolitan Statistical Areas (MSAs) are analyzed. The newsletter includes tables and graphs of labor force information for the state, MSAs, and Micropolitan Areas. Data tables are also provided with estimated non-agricultural employment by industry, hours and earnings of manufacturing production workers, and the consumer price index.

If the month and year you need is not available on this website, please contact the Research and Statistics office at 615-741-2284 to request a copy. You may also e-mail requests to the Tennessee Department of Labor and Workforce Development via WIRED.Info@tn.gov Please include your mailing address, telephone number and the month and year of the report.
