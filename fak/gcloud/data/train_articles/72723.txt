First Lady's "Walk with Walker" encourages people to live a healthier lifestyle

Wisconsin's First Lady is encouraging people to live a healthier lifestyle.

Dozens of people joined Tonette Walker for her first "Walk With Walker" event of the year.

This morning's gathering was at Veteran's Memorial Park in Port Washington.

Tonette was joined by her husband Governor Scott Walker as they explored one of the park's scenic trails.

"It's a great way to get out and see some of our great tourism attractions across the state of Wisconsin."
