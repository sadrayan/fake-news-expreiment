हिंदी संस्करण KHARAI CAMEL

National Bureau of Animal Genetic Resources

Karnal-132001 (Haryana) Kharai camel is found in coastal part of Kachchh region of Gujarat. These camels are well adapted to both dry-land as well as costal ecosystems. They have excellent swimming capacity in sea water and graze mainly on mangrove and other saline species. Kharai camel can thrive on high saline water and tolerate high TDS. BACK
