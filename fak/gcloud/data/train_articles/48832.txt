Trump gives fourth-quarter salary to Transportation Department

President Donald Trump has donated his salary from the latest quarter to the Department of Transportation’s push to improve U.S. infrastructure, the White House announced Tuesday.

“Today, the president is proud to donate his fourth-quarter 2017 salary to the Department of Transportation to support their programs to rebuild and modernize our crumbling infrastructure,” White House press secretary Sarah Huckabee Sanders said at the press briefing.

Transportation Secretary Elaine Chao was on hand to accept the donation, and stressed the importance of rebuilding U.S. roads, bridges and more.

The president has vowed to donate his entire annual salary of $400,000 while he is in office.
