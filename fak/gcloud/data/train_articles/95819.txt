The Notebook 2 Filming in Jacksonville, North Carolina, Plot Details Released – WBN 10 News
HOLLYWOOD, California – The very popular 2004 romantic drama film The Notebook is finally getting a sequel. Producers of the upcoming film confirmed the news today and announced that filming will begin in Jacksonville, North Carolina this summer.

A screenplay written by Nicholas Sparks was reportedly approved just last week and Nick Cassavetes is said to have signed on again as director. Also, Ryan Gosling and Rachel McAdams will return as their characters, Noah and Allie. Few details of the upcoming film have been released so far but the short description released this morning shows that several years after their wedding the couple have moved to Jacksonville, North Carolina for a period of time before eventually returning to Seabrook. The film will take place primarily in the Jacksonville area and will film on location there as well. A casting call for extras will reportedly take place in the Jacksonville area in a couple of months.

Fans of the original film were obviously very excited about the news with #thenotebook2 very quickly becoming one of the most popular hashtags of the day. When asked why they’ve chosen to make a sequel after so many years, the producers said, “People want sequels to all of the great movies from the past and they love remakes too! I can definitely see in maybe 20 years doing a remake of The Notebook, maybe this time with an all black cast, or all Asian cast… something to mix it up, ya know?”
