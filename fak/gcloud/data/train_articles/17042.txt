1

2 IMPORTANT NOTES

3

4 This sheet was produced using data provided by Fortune. The information provided included a list of Fortune 1000 companies from its latest listing and the address of each company's headquarters.

5

6 Population estimates used for each city came from the U.S. Census Bureau. The population value shown for each city is the most recent available estimate.

7

8 This data is NOT a definitive ranking of current values for Fortune 500 or Fortune 1000 companies per capita, given that some headquarters have changed locations since the list was published and population counts are based on estimates from the Census Bureau.

10 TABLE OF CONTENTS

11

12 Sheet Title Description

13 Population counts This sheet shows population estimates from the U.S. Census Bureau for each city with at least one Fortune 1000 headquarters.

14 Fortune 500 List, locations This sheet includes the most recent Fortune 500 list, which includes each company's name, position on the list and the address of its headquarters. It also includes population count for each city.

15 Fortune 1000 List, locations This sheet includes the most recent Fortune 1000 list, which includes each company's name, position on the list and the address of its headquarters. It also includes population count for each city.

16 Fortune 500 per capita This sheet shows calculations for the number of Fortune 500 companies per capita for each city/township with at least one headquarters, as indicated by the data provided by Fortune. Calculations for per capita values follow this forumula: (# of companies/population) * 100,000.

17 Fortune 1000 per capita This sheet shows calculations for the number of Fortune 1000 companies per capita for each city/township with at least one headquarters, as indicated by the data provided by Fortune. Calculations for per capita values follow this forumula: (# of companies/population) * 100,000.

18 Fortune 500 per capita, + 100,000 This sheet shows calculations for the number of Fortune 500 companies per capita for each city/township with at least one headquarters and a population of more than 100,000 people. Calculations for per capita values follow the formula above.

19 Fortune 1000 per capita, + 100,000 This sheet shows calculations for the number of Fortune 1000 companies per capita for each city/township with at least one headquarters and a population of more than 100,000 people. Calculations for per capita values follow the formula above.
