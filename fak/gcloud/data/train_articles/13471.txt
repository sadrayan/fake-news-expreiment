Watch: Kaine, Stewart face off in first debate
Democratic U.S. Sen. Tim Kaine and Republican challenger Corey Stewart will square off for their first debate on Saturday. You can watch the debate here.

WASHINGTON – Democratic U.S. Sen. Tim Kaine and Republican challenger Corey Stewart are revving up for their first debate.

Kaine and Stewart will face off on Saturday at the Virginia Bar Association summer meeting in Hot Springs. The debate starts at 11 a.m., moderated by PBS NewsHour’s Judy Woodruff.

Watch a livestream on this page when it gets underway.
