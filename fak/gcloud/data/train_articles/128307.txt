Big Problem For Pirates As Mokwena Takes Over As Head Coach After Micho Resignation
Orlando Pirates head coach Milutin Sredojevic says the Buccaneers were punished for making ‘silly’ mistakes in their Nedbank Cup defeat to Black Leopards.

However, according to reports emanating from an unconfirmed source stated that the coach is said to have tender his resignation to the chairman of the club, Dr Irvin Khoza.

According to the source, Pirates is said to have given Mokwena, the assistant coach an express order to stir the affairs of the club as the acting Coach, until the solve the issues surrounding the club in no distant time.

However, Trendsdaily Source who spoke to us on phone state the coach is said to be fed up with the some of the internal decisions
