The Four Terrorists Who Boarded Flight 93
United Airlines Flight 93 was hijacked by four individuals identified as Ziad Samir Jarrah, Saeed al Ghamdi, Ahmed Ibrahim A. al Haznawi, and Ahmed Abdullah al Nami, armed with cutting instruments and stating that they had a box containing a bomb. The hijackers of United Airlines Flight 93 were led by Jarrah, the only hijacker of this group trained as a pilot.
