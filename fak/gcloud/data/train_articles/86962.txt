1 dead, 2 wounded in Cypress shootings
CYPRESS – One man was killed and two were injured during a shooting Thursday night at a Cypress hotel, police said.

Officers responding to reports of shots fired at Cypress Lodge on Lincoln Avenue about 9:30 p.m. were directed to a room where three men were suffering from gunshot wounds, Cypress police Lt. Jim Olson said.

One man was pronounced dead at the scene, Olson said.

A second had been shot in the torso and was taken to UCI Medical Center in Orange in critical condition. A third had been shot in the leg and was taken to UCI Medical Center with what are believed to be non-life-threatening injuries.

Police arrested a fourth man on suspicion of murder, attempted murder and assault with a deadly weapon. The man was detained outside the hotel, Olson said.

Detectives found a handgun believed to have been used during the shootings inside the hotel room where the men were found, Olson said.

Police have not released the men’s names.

Lincoln Avenue was closed in both directions between Walker Avenue and Valley View Street as police went over the scene.
