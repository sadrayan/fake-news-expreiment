At least 21 were killed in an explosion in Mar Girgis church in Egypt
Description

epa05898852 Security personnel investigate the scene of a bomb explosion inside Mar Girgis church in Tanta, 90km north of Cairo, Egypt, 09 April 2017. According to the Egyptian Health Ministry, at least 21 were killed and 69 injured in a bomb explosion at Mar Girgis Coptic church in the central delta city of Tanta during the Palm Sunday mass.
