Parsley Coffin Renner LLP
As a former Commissioner of the Public Utility Commission of Texas, Ms. Parsley focuses her practice on energy-related legal and consulting services in Texas and the Southwest Power Pool. Ms. Parsley is also a former Solicitor General of Texas, is Board Certified in Civil Appellate Law, and was an Adjunct Professor of Law teaching Appellate Practice and Procedure at the University of Texas School of Law. She also enjoys an appellate practice in addition to her energy practice.
