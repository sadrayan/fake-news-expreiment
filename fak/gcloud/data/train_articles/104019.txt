31 Insects So Scary You’ll Never Go Outside Again

There are some really creepy critters out there – here’s a gallery of some of the scariest…

1. Georgian Speekle

Last year in Georgia, a homeowner got the fright of their life when they found this spiky, cylinder-shaped monster skulking in their garage. It later appeared in more Georgian homes, causing a lot of fear. Experts, who named it the ‘Georgian Speekle’ said it wasn’t poisonous, but they couldn’t give much more information as they’d never seen the breed before.
